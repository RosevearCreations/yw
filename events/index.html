See how Devil n Dove handles recurring events, markets, and pop-ups

Some pieces make the most sense online, some work better through pickup-friendly conversations, and some belong at a local market table where condition, scale, and provenance can be discussed in person.

Handmade pieces

Workshop-made items can be shown at events when buyers want to see scale, finish, or colour in person before they commit.

Vintage and collectible stock

Old tools, curiosities, antiquities, and oddities often benefit from direct questions about wear, repairs, era clues, and pickup practicality.

Local Southern Ontario support

Event and pickup pages help local buyers understand whether a piece is shipped, reserved, shown at a market, or linked out to another sales channel.

What buyers can expect

  • Clear item origin: handmade, vintage, collectible, or pre-built.
  • Condition notes kept plain and visible.
  • Pickup or event discussions routed through Contact when timing matters.
  • Sale-channel clarity when an item also lives on Facebook Marketplace or another venue.

What we can bring to events

Handmade jewelry, creative experiments, workshop-made pieces, and selected found/vintage stock can all fit the event side of Devil n Dove, but not every item belongs in every venue.
