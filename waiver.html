Service Requirements & Waiver

Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing (Norfolk & Oxford Counties, Ontario). These terms are acknowledged during booking.

Driveway required Power + water required Local bylaws
This page is general information and not legal advice.

1) Work area requirements

Driveway / work space
  • A safe, accessible driveway or equivalent work area is required.
  • We must be able to park and work safely around the vehicle.
  • We may decline or reschedule if the site is unsafe (ice, tight space, hazards, etc.).
Water + Power
  • Customer must provide a working outdoor water source and electrical outlet.
  • If water/power is unavailable, additional charges may apply (or the appointment may be rescheduled).
Weather
  • Mobile detailing is weather dependent. Severe weather may require rescheduling.

2) Environmental & bylaw responsibility

Mobile detailing can involve soaps/cleaners and rinsing. Local bylaws vary by municipality.

Customer responsibility
  • Customer confirms that washing/detailing at their location is permitted under local bylaws/HOA rules.
  • Customer accepts responsibility for any municipal/HOA issues related to on-site washing, runoff, or chemical use.
  • Customer will inform us of any site restrictions before the appointment.

3) Scheduling & capacity

One vehicle per day (usually)
  • We normally schedule one vehicle per day unless two half-day services fit the schedule.
  • If a vehicle condition requires extra time, we may adjust scope or reschedule with you.

4) Deposits, cancellations, and no-refund items

Deposit
  • A deposit is required to reserve your time slot.
Cancellations
  • Cancellation fees may apply depending on notice given.
  • Last-minute cancellations reduce available service time for other customers.
Gift Certificates
  • Gift certificates are valid for 1 year from purchase date.
  • No refunds on gift certificate purchases.

5) Vehicle condition, pre-existing damage

  • Customer agrees to disclose any known issues (delicate trim, failing clear coat, electrical issues, etc.).
  • We are not responsible for pre-existing damage or wear that becomes visible during cleaning (oxidation, scratches, stains, etc.).
  • Some add-ons are “quote required” due to vehicle condition variability.

6) Contact

If you have questions before booking, email us: info@rosiedazzlers.ca or rosiedazzlers@gmail.com.

During booking, you acknowledge these requirements via checkboxes (driveway, power/water, bylaw, cancellation).
