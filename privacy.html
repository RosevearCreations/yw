Privacy

How we handle your information.

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone).
  • Service address and booking details.
  • Vehicle details you provide (year/make/model and optional photo link).
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe (we do not store your full card number).

How we use it

  • To schedule and perform your detailing service.
  • To contact you about your appointment.
  • To provide receipts and booking confirmation.
  • To show you your job progress (if enabled) via a private link.

Sharing

  • We share data with service providers only as needed (e.g., Stripe for payments, Supabase for data storage).
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Progress links

If progress tracking is enabled, you may receive a link like /progress?token=…. Anyone with that link can view the progress feed. Keep it private.

Contact

Questions? Email us at info@rosiedazzlers.ca.
