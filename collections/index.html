Why this page exists

Handmade and found stock are different

Handmade listings should lead with making story, materials, and workshop process. Vintage and collectible stock should lead with provenance clues, condition, era, and channel clarity.

Shoppers need context sooner

Collection landing pages help buyers choose the right path before they compare one-off pieces, linked marketplace finds, and workshop-made creations together.

Local trust still matters

Southern Ontario buyers often want to know whether an item is shipped, pickup-friendly, or routed to another marketplace before they reach checkout.
