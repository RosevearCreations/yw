About Rosie Dazzlers

Rosie Dazzlers Mobile Auto Detailing is the brainchild of our children, an eco-conscious cleaning service that brings convenience and car care to your doorstep. Serving Norfolk and Oxford counties, Rosie Dazzlers is more than just a detailing service; it's a commitment to quality, sustainability, and community.

Our team prides itself on transforming vehicles with meticulous attention to detail, using environmentally friendly products when possible to protect your car and the planet.

Mobile auto detailing brings the full detailing experience to your location, offering convenience and personalized attention without the need to leave your home or office. We focus on clear pricing, clear expectations, and a booking flow that makes it easy to reserve a date.

What we do
Mobile auto detailing with packages for exterior, interior, and full detail services.
Where we serve
Norfolk County and Oxford County, with service brought directly to the customer.
How we work
Deposit-secured bookings, clear package pricing, add-ons, and one vehicle per day in most cases.

Services offered

  • Exterior washing and drying — hand washing the exterior, including wheels and tires.
  • Waxing and polishing — protection and shine, with polishing for light swirls where applicable.
  • Interior cleaning — vacuuming, wipe-down, disinfecting, and interior care.
  • Engine bay cleaning — light cleaning to remove dirt and grease for a cleaner appearance.
  • Detailing extras — add-ons like pet hair removal, coatings, and specialty services.

Benefits of mobile detailing

  • Convenience — the service comes to you.
  • Personalized service — attention to your vehicle’s specific needs.
  • Flexible scheduling — pick times that work better for your schedule.
  • Better techniques — professional tools and products for deeper cleaning and protection.

What customers should know before booking

  • A driveway or suitable work area is required.
  • Customers must provide access to power and water, or accept additional charges where applicable.
  • Deposits are required to secure an appointment.
  • Cancellation fees may apply.
  • Customers accept responsibility for any local bylaw or runoff restrictions at their location.
  • We typically limit bookings to one vehicle per day unless it’s a half-day job.

Simple maintenance tips

  • Wash your vehicle regularly to reduce grime buildup.
  • Clean spills and debris quickly to avoid stains and odours.
  • Park in the shade where possible to reduce UV exposure.
  • Regular upkeep helps preserve value and finish between full details.
